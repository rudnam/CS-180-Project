CS 180: Predictive model of household income using clustering

Project Summary

In this project, a dataset based on the Family Income and Expenditure Survey (FIES) in the Philippines is put through K-means clustering. Through clustering, we aim to figure out possible classifications for households through the said dataset. We suspect that the clusters will be correlated with income or geographic areas from which a household belongs.
